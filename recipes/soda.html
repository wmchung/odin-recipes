Japanese Cream Soda

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp melon syrup
  • ice cubes
  • 3/4 cup club soda
  • vanilla ice cream
  • maraschino cherries(optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Put ice cubes into the glass and add 3 Tbsp melon syrup
  3. Add 3/4 cup club soda.
  4. Mix well with a spoon.
  5. Top with vanilla ice cream.
  6. Garnish with a cherry.
