Carrot Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 sprig Italian Parsley
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp sugar

    • Steps

    1. Gather all the ingredients.
    2. Using a peeler, peel the carrot into thin sheets, and then cut them in halves.
    3. Cut the thin sheets of carrots into julieene strips and put them in a medium bowl.
    4. Add all the seasonings.
    5. Let them marinate for at least 1 hours before serving. The carrot salad will keep for 5-6 days in the refrigerator.
